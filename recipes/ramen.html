Struggle Ramen Deluxe

Description

This ramen is super easy to make and hits the spot. Some would even consider it peak frugal living.

Ingredients

  • 1 ramen package of desired brand and flavor
  • 1/4 cup of diced mushroom
  • 3 pieces of Spam, cut to 1/4inch thickness
  • 1/4 cup of chopped green onion
  • Optional: 1 egg overeasy

Steps

  1. Start to boil about 4 cups of water in a small pot.
  2. While water waits to come to a boil, preheat a skillet for Spam and mushrooms. Set at medium-high heat.
  3. Cut Spam, green onions and mushrooms. Set aside.
  4. Once skillet is hot, add the strips of Spam and fry till light brown or desired doneness.
  5. If water is boiling, add noodles from ramen packet. Leave seasoning to the side.
  6. Briefly warm up mushrooms in the same skillet as the Spam.
  7. Add ramen to bowl with half the water remaining. Add the seasoning packet, green onions, mushrooms and spam.
  8. Top with overeasy egg if desired.
