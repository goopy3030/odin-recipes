Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

This greasy, cheesy, slightly spicy dip may only be edible for one day out of the year, but can make the super bowl watchable.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole rotisserie chicken
  • 1 cup of Frank's RedHot or other desired sauce.
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup ranch or 1/2 cup ranch and 1/2 cup blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup green onion
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Crackers
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar

Steps

  1. Begin carving up rotisserie chicken. Share wings and drumsticks with party or yourself to satiate desires.
  2. Chop 1/2 yellow onion to small dices.
  3. Sweat onions in large cast iron skillet on medium heat with butter. Add in chicken and hot sauce. Stir. Note: It is a good idea that the cream cheese is softened before next step.
  4. Add in cream cheese and stir. Add ranch and optional blue cheese. Mix well.
  5. Once ingredients have had a chance to get to know eachother, top with cheddar. Transfer skillet to oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Allow 30-40 minutes for cheese to crisp.
  6. Take skillet out of oven and rest for at least 3 minutes. Top with green onion and serve with crackers of choice.
